## Jimmy (Miao Zhang)

#### Jimmy is a computational researcher, architect, and urban designer whose work sits at the intersection of machine learning, smart city/infrastructure, housing/collective living, and historic preservation. His current research focuses on: (1) developing computational methods (LLM and machine learning) to visualize buildable deltas and anticipate future development potential in metropolitan regions; and (2) exploring neighborhood renewal strategies through data-informed analyses of urban adjacency conditions.
####
#### Jimmy is the co-founder of Commons Office, a research-minded design practice that embraces a trans-scalar and trans-disciplinary approach across architecture, urbanism, engineering, and artificial intelligence. The practice views design as a collective social agency that can empower the commons and advance the common good through critical inquiry, collaboration with communities, professionals, and stakeholders, and the application of computational methods.
####
#### Jimmy is a current MS in Computational Science and Engineering student at Georgia Tech. He also received his Master in Architecture in Urban Design (MAUD) from Harvard University and a Bachelor of Architecture from Inner Mongolia University of Technology.
